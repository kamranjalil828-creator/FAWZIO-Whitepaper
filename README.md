# PROSPERA
### Intelligence for Everyone. Free. Forever.

> "Prospera exists because nothing is impossible without money."

Prospera is a peer-to-peer AI network where human beings collectively 
become the infrastructure for each other's intelligence.

No servers. No subscriptions. No hardware requirements.
Just people — connected — lifting each other up through what they already have.

---

## The Problem
Millions of people cannot access AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
Not because they lack intelligence. But because someone decided thinking should cost money.

## The Solution
Prospera uses the idle bandwidth of its own users as the network.
Everyone who joins contributes what they already have.
Everyone who contributes earns access.
The poorest person who joins makes every other poor person richer.
