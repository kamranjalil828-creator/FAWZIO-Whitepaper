The Problem

The world has tools.
Most people can't
afford them.

01

AI is paywalled for billions

ChatGPT Plus. Claude Pro. Gemini Advanced. The most powerful AI tools in history cost more per month than many people's entire food budget. The democratization of intelligence stopped at a credit card form.

◈
02

Cloud computing is someone else's server

Every AI service runs on infrastructure owned by a handful of corporations. They control the costs, the access, the rules. What they give, they can take away. Millions depend on goodwill, not infrastructure they own.

◈
03

Talent has no geography

The student in Lahore is as brilliant as the student in London. But the London student has $200/month tools, warm intros, and investor networks. The Lahore student has a phone and a dream. Fawzio levels the field.

◈
04

Free tiers are designed to fail

Limited to 10 requests. Then a wall. Then an upsell. "Free" is a marketing trick, not a commitment. No one builds their future on a foundation that was engineered to frustrate them into paying.

◈
How It Works

Your idle internet
becomes
your payment.

No money. No GPU. No server. No data sold. Fawzio is powered by the collective unused bandwidth of its own users — a living, breathing network owned by no one and available to everyone.

01

Install Fawzio

Any phone or laptop. No special hardware. Works on the most basic device you already own.

02

Share Idle Bandwidth

Your internet sits mostly idle. Fawzio uses that wasted portion silently in the background. You never feel a difference.

03

Network Thinks Together

Thousands of contributors form one collective intelligence. No central server. No single owner. People helping people.

04

You Receive Answers

Free. Unlimited. Every time. Forever. No expiry. No "upgrade to continue." No catch. This is the promise.
